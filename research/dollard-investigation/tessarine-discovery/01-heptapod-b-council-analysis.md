**The algebra where h^2 = +1 and h is NOT -1.**

- e_+ = (1 + j_h)/2

with e_+^2 = e_+, e_-^2 = e_-, e_+ * e_- = 0, and e_+ + e_- = 1.

The exponential exp(j_h * delta) = cosh(delta) + j_h * sinh(delta). This is EXACTLY Dollard's h-operator: it produces hyperbolic functions. But j_h is NOT -1; it is a genuine independent element.

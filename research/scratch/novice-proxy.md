# Novice Proxy Test

## Concept 1: Action in Physics (Q = Psi * Phi has units of action)

**12-Year-Old Explanation:**
Imagine you push a ball across a table. You use a certain amount of energy over a certain amount of time. "Action" is a way of measuring the TOTAL effort -- it is energy multiplied by the time you spent using it. The units are joule-seconds: how many joules for how many seconds.

In electricity, there are two basic things: the "push" (voltage) and the "flow" (current). Magnetic flux is like voltage accumulated over time (volt-seconds). Electric charge is like current accumulated over time (ampere-seconds). When you multiply them together: (volt-seconds) * (ampere-seconds) = (volts * amps) * (seconds * seconds) = watts * seconds-squared = joules * seconds. That is action.

So when Dollard says Q = Psi * Phi (flux times charge), he is calculating the "total effort" of the electromagnetic system. This is not a new idea -- physicists have called this "action" since the 1700s.

**Status**: PASSED. The explanation survives without hand-waving.

## Concept 2: Why h = -1 is the only possibility

**12-Year-Old Explanation:**
Suppose someone tells you "I have a special number h, and its first power equals -1." Well, any number raised to the first power IS that number. So h = h^1 = -1. That is not special at all; it is just -1 wearing a costume.

Now they also say "h squared equals 1." Well, (-1) * (-1) = 1. That checks out. But it does not make h interesting -- it just confirms it is -1.

**Status**: PASSED. The logical necessity is transparent to anyone who understands exponents.

## Concept 3: The Lagrangian and Action Principle

**12-Year-Old Explanation:**
Nature is lazy. Among all the paths a ball could take from point A to point B, it takes the one that minimizes a quantity called "action." Action is calculated by adding up (kinetic energy minus potential energy) at every moment along the path. The function (kinetic minus potential) is called the Lagrangian.

In electric circuits: the energy stored in an inductor (coil of wire) is like kinetic energy. The energy stored in a capacitor (two plates) is like potential energy. The Lagrangian for a circuit is inductor energy minus capacitor energy.

The "coordinates" of the circuit are how much charge has flowed. The "momentum" is how much magnetic flux is linked. Just like position*momentum has units of action in mechanics, charge*flux has units of action in circuits.

**Status**: PASSED with caveat. The "nature is lazy" simplification hides the Euler-Lagrange machinery, but the essential insight (charge and flux are conjugate variables whose product is action) survives.

## Concept 4: Split-Complex Numbers

**12-Year-Old Explanation:**
You know the imaginary number i where i^2 = -1. There is a different kind of "special number" j where j^2 = +1. You might think j must be 1 or -1, but no: j is a NEW thing, like how i is a new thing different from all regular numbers. The numbers a + bj (where a and b are normal numbers) are called "split-complex" numbers.

The catch: these numbers have a weird property. You can find two non-zero numbers that multiply to zero: (1+j) * (1-j) = 1 - j^2 = 1 - 1 = 0. In regular numbers, that cannot happen. This makes split-complex numbers useful for some things (describing certain physical transformations) but tricky for others.

**Status**: PASSED. The zero-divisor property is correctly identified as the key distinguishing feature.

## Concept 5: What Dollard Claims vs. What Is Real

**12-Year-Old Explanation:**
Imagine someone discovers that the area of a rectangle is width times height. They say: "I have discovered a revolutionary new formula that replaces all of geometry!" The formula (area = width * height) is correct. But calling it "revolutionary" and claiming it "replaces geometry" is wrong -- everyone already knew this.

Dollard found that flux times charge equals action. This is correct (it follows from the units). He claims this replaces E = mc^2. That is like saying "area = width * height" replaces the Pythagorean theorem. The formula is real; the revolutionary claim is not.

**Status**: PASSED. The analogy correctly separates dimensional correctness from novelty.

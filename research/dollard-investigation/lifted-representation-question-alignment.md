You project down to the standard functions only when you need a final answer:                                                                                                          
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 
  cos = u - v       (may be ill-conditioned near resonance)                                                                                                                                                                                                                      
  sin = x - y       (may be ill-conditioned near π)                                                                                                                                                                                                                              
  cosh = u + v      (always well-conditioned)
